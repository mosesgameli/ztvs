# Support for ZTVS

We want to ensure you have the best experience with the Zero Trust Vulnerability Scanner (ZTVS). Here are the various ways to get support:

## Community Support

The primary way to get help is through the community:

*   **GitHub Issues**: For bug reports, feature requests, and general questions, please use our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/mosesgameli/ztvs/issues).
*   **Discussions**: Check the [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/mosesgameli/ztvs/discussions) tab for community conversations and tips.

## Reporting Bugs

When reporting a bug, please include:

1.  A clear and concise description of the issue.
2.  Your operating system and ZTVS version.
3.  Steps to reproduce the problem.
4.  Relevant logs (from `stderr`) or screenshots.

## Security Issues

Please **do not** report security vulnerabilities through public channels. Refer to our [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for instructions on how to report security issues privately.

## Professional Support

Currently, ZTVS is a community-driven project. If you require professional support or enterprise-level SLAs, please contact the maintainers at [ztvs-support@mosesgameli.com](mailto:ztvs-support@mosesgameli.com) to discuss options.
